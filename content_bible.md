# SHP Content Bible v1.0

## The Golden Rule

Every documentary must tell a hidden story where nature changed history.

Nature is never just a subject.

Nature is always the reason history changed.

Why does this story matter?

How did it change history?

Why don't most people know this?

Why should someone click immediately?

A topic is approved only if it makes people say:

"I've never heard this before."

If not...

Reject the topic.
