
# SHP Brand Bible v1.0

# Brand Name

The Secret History of Plants

---

# Tagline

The Hidden Stories That Changed the World

---

# Mission

Reveal the hidden stories of plants, fungi, forests, spices, medicines, and nature that silently changed human civilization.

Every documentary must leave the viewer saying:

"I had no idea this changed history."

---

# What SHP Is

SHP is not a botany channel.

SHP is not a mushroom channel.

SHP is a cinematic documentary brand about the hidden history of nature.

Nature is always the main character.

History is always the consequence.

---

# Our Promise

Every video must uncover a real hidden story.

Every documentary must combine:

- History
- Science
- Archaeology
- Mythology
- Human Civilization
